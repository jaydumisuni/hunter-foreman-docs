# Judge FAQ

## Why not just use ChatGPT?

ChatGPT can answer questions. Hunter Foreman turns business requests into owned work. It creates tasks, selects workflows, updates dashboards, dispatches to connected apps, and escalates to humans.

## Why not Jira, Trello, or Monday.com?

Those tools organize tasks after someone creates them. Hunter Foreman focuses on the missing step before that: converting messy customer requests into structured work automatically.

## What is ROSE?

ROSE is the AI receptionist layer. She receives and structures the request before passing it to Foreman.

## What is Foreman?

Foreman is the operations layer. It reasons about the request, chooses a workflow, creates a task, and keeps humans in control.

## What is open source?

The public demo app, receiver app, bridge contract, docs, and public-safe routing examples.

## What stays private?

Private Hunter internals, private prompts, production credentials, client data, business-sensitive workflows, and device repair/recovery modules.

## Why AMD?

The roadmap includes local deployment on AMD Ryzen mini PCs for small businesses that want an always-on AI operations assistant they can own and control.

## How does this become a business?

Possible paths include hosted plans, local business appliances, support/setup services, workflow plugins, and industry-specific Foreman packs.
