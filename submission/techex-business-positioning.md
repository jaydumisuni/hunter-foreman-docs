# TechEx Business Positioning

## Submission Angle

Hunter Foreman is a business operations platform powered by AI.

It helps businesses receive customer requests, convert them into owned work, route them to the correct workflow, dispatch them to connected applications, and keep humans informed through visible status.

## Why It Fits Business Deployment

The project is designed around deployable business value rather than demo-only chat.

Business-facing strengths:

- AI receptionist for customer intake
- operations foreman for routing
- dashboard for visibility
- external app connection
- task state tracking
- human escalation when needed
- Docker-based local setup
- public/private boundary for safe adoption

## Demo Story

```text
Visitor asks for help
   ↓
ROSE captures the request
   ↓
Foreman chooses the workflow
   ↓
Task appears in dashboard
   ↓
Connected app receives it
   ↓
Business sees status and acknowledgement
```

## What Judges Should Notice

- This is not a toy assistant.
- It can support real business workflows.
- The receiver app proves the system can connect to external apps.
- The dashboard makes work visible.
- The public repos are understandable and runnable.
- The same architecture can later support Events, Support, Sales, Pay, and other business systems.

## Strongest Line

Hunter Foreman gives small businesses an AI operations layer they can actually deploy, not just a chatbot they have to babysit.
